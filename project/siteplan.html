Site Plan - Homemade Baking Store

Proposed Logo

This is the proposed logo to represent the Sweet Creations brand.

Site Name

Proposed Name: Sweet Creations

Reason: This name reflects the homemade and delightful nature of the desserts offered. It is memorable, appealing, and directly tied to the theme of baking.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an online presence for Sweet Creations, showcasing a catalog of homemade desserts, offering a platform for custom orders, sharing baking tips and recipes, and enabling customers to contact the business easily.

Scenarios

  • How can I place a custom order for a birthday cake?
  • What baking tips can help me improve my homemade desserts?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #d2691e (used for headings and accents).
  • Secondary Color: #f5a623 (used for backgrounds and buttons).
  • Background: #fff9f5 (used for the main page background).

Typography

  • Headings: "Georgia", serif.
  • Body Text: "Arial", sans-serif.
